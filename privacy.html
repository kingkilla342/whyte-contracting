Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025 · Whyte Contracting LLC · Copiague, NY 11726

1. Information We Collect

When you submit a quote request or job application on this website, we collect the information you voluntarily provide — including your name, phone number, email address, company name, and project details. We do not collect any information automatically through cookies or tracking scripts beyond what your browser sends with standard HTTP requests.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to respond to your inquiry, prepare a project estimate, or review your job application. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Form Submission Processing

Form submissions on this site are processed through Formsubmit.co, a third-party form handling service. Your submission data is transmitted to Formsubmit's servers and forwarded to our business email. Please review Formsubmit's privacy policy for details on how they handle data in transit.

4. Data Retention

Inquiry and application information is retained in our email inbox for as long as necessary to fulfill the business purpose for which it was submitted — typically the duration of a project relationship or hiring process. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us directly.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal information we hold about you. To make such a request, contact us at info@whytecontracting.com or by mail at P.O. Box 116, Copiague, NY 11726.

6. Security

This website is served over HTTPS. We take reasonable precautions to protect submitted information, but no internet transmission is 100% secure. We recommend you do not submit sensitive financial or legal information through this contact form.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
