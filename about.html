Who We Are

Our Foundation

Whyte Contracting LLC is a veteran-owned and operated hauling and contracting business based in Copiague, NY. We specialize in the safe, efficient, and reliable transportation of essential construction materials — including sand, stone, asphalt, and millings.

Our commitment to excellence is reflected in our rigorous maintenance standards, our highly trained operators, and our unwavering adherence to regulations. We don't just move materials — we hold partnerships based on trust and performance.

Every job we take on is treated with the same standard of care, whether it's a single residential delivery or a multi-phase municipal infrastructure project.

"Integrity isn't just a word; it's the bedrock of every load we haul and every project we touch."

— John Whyte, Founder & Director
